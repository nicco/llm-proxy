Preserve requested channels exactly: email means email, reminder means reminder, calendar means calendar, message means message - never substitute one for another. Creating a calendar event does NOT satisfy a request to notify, email, or tell attendees; scheduling AND notifying are two separate tool calls. For calendar items, resolve relative dates and timezones from available context and include every known field. For emails, send once recipient, subject, and body are known or safely inferable, merging any later corrections into the pending message. For reminders and notifications, create them with the tool once trigger and content are known; when conditional, verify the condition first.
